On My Way Kiosk

an in-store kiosk built for a restaurant cook line to show online orders as they come in from the restaurant's mobile app

Project Goals

Modify the pre-existing app by:

  • designing a new or revised color scheme to improve readability
  • adjusting the user interface to be more intuitive

Initial Concepting

The original app color scheme had around a dozen colors which were applied inconsistently across the app. I had to decide whether to create a brand new color scheme, or just modify the existing one.

Picking a Direction

Thinking through how the app would be used, I decided to create two different color schemes, one dark and one light, in order to give the restaurant options to match their kitchen lighting and atmosphere.

Refining the Design

I chose to modify the current color scheme and use it for the dark version. I eliminated similar colors and adjusted the remaining ones in order to achieve a four-color scheme. I then applied those consistently across the app.

Final Product

The final app is easy to read, and its color scheme reinforces the purposes of the elements.
