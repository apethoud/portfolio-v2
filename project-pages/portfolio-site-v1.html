Personal Portfolio Site v1.0

the very first version of my personal portfolio site

Project Goals

Create a personal portfolio site that:

  • is mobile-friendly
  • demonstrates the work I'm capable of
  • represents me well as a web developer

Initial Concepting

I started by brainstorming a personal logo to represent me.

Picking a Direction

After I decided on a logo, I worked to incorporate similar shapes into the design of the site. In particular, I created section breaks set at different angles.

Refining the Design

I had to tweak how I set the spacing of elements on the site due to the unique shapes, and then make sure those were implemented well when accessed from a mobile device or tablet.

Final Product

The final site demonstrates a unique approach to design, coupled with the ability to build the site architecture to suit.
